ABOUT

DMITRY ITSYKSON
MATHEMATICIAN / SENIOR RESEARCHER
I work at Laboratory of Mathematical Logic of Steklov Institute of Mathematics at St.Petersburg. Basically I'm a researcher in the field of psychology and psychotherapy. I'm dying to figure out the mechanisms of how people think and feel.
At the same time, I work as an IT HR, currently as a freelance IT recruiter. I consider my HR activities more as a hobby, though this is my full-time job. I value intelligence and sincerity. I'm deeply interested in what people are. I love analyzing things.
